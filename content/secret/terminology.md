^viral

**Viral** in the context of my content is anything over 20k views in a short period of time. I'm not actually famous by any means so I give myself these little wins when I get them.

^soul-friends

**Soul friends** is still somewhat of a new term to me, but the way I understand it is that it's the type of friendship where there's a truly deep human connection - one where both parties are sincerely invested in the growth and nurturing of the other.
