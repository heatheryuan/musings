I’m Heather 👋 – code monkey at Netflix, serial content creator, and chronic overthinker.

#### Welcome to *musings* ✨
Think of this as my brain on the internet. 

Here you can find short thought nuggets, weekly retros, curated recipes and reviews, full-blown philosophical essays, and everything in between.

#### Why *musings*?
Something I used to struggle with was detangling all my thoughts from each other and feeling convicted in any of them. This was troublesome not only for my self-belief but also my communication with others.

I started writing as a way to practice putting my thoughts to words and defending them in an intelligible way. I also find a lot of meaning in externalizing my thoughts through the process of creation.

With that said, I also wanted a space to just let my mind be known - from the silly to the half-baked to the formalized.

Take a look around, and thanks for being here :)
